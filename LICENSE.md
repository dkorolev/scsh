## Third-party licenses

None — `scsh` is distributable under plain MIT, end to end. The session
browser's player and the pages generated by `scsh export-cast` embed the same
first-party clean-room player — born here as `scsh-cast-player`, now the MIT
`beecast-player` crate (embedded directly and via the MIT `beecast-page`
crate). Every dependency is either MIT
outright or dual-licensed with an `OR` under which **scsh elects MIT**; no
Apache-only (or otherwise non-MIT-electable) material ships in the binary or
in any generated page.

Tests keep all of this true: `licenses::every_dependency_is_usable_under_plain_mit`
audits the whole lockfile's SPDX expressions, and the player/export tests assert
no third-party license marker appears in the browser assets or exported pages.
